# The Quiet Page 1.1.3

This update adds five quiet soundscapes for writing: Rain, Forest, Café,
Lo-fi, and Deep Space.

- Switch moods instantly with a soft half-second crossfade.
- Adjust ambient volume independently from typewriter sounds.
- The selected mood is remembered but starts paused on every launch.
- On the Write tab, ambience gently softens after eight seconds of inactivity
  and returns when typing resumes.
- Every sound remains local and works fully offline.
