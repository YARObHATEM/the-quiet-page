About

The Quiet Page

A private offline writing journal for Windows.

—

Everything stays on your computer.

The Quiet Page does not connect to the internet. Ever. Your entries are saved only on this device, in a folder you control. There are no accounts, no cloud, no analytics, no telemetry — nothing leaves your machine. Your words are yours alone.
